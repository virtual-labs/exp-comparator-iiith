### Textbooks

**1. Digital Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Chapter 4 (Combinational Logic) and Chapter 5 (Synchronous Sequential Logic)<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of digital comparator circuits, magnitude comparison, and combinational logic design._

**2. Digital Logic and Computer Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano - Chapter 4<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of digital logic design including binary comparison and magnitude comparator implementations._

**3. Digital Electronics: Principles, Devices and Applications**<br/>
BY: Anil K. Maini - Chapter 6<br/>
_Practical approach to digital circuit design with detailed analysis of comparator circuits and applications._

**4. Digital Systems: Principles and Applications**<br/>
BY: Ronald J. Tocci, Neal S. Widmer, and Gregory L. Moss - Chapter 7<br/>
_Excellent coverage of digital comparison operations and magnitude comparator circuit implementation._

**5. Computer Organization and Design**<br/>
BY: David A. Patterson and John L. Hennessy - Chapter 3 (Arithmetic for Computers)<br/>
_Hardware perspective on comparison operations and their implementation in processors and control units._

**6. Digital Electronics: A Practical Approach with VHDL**<br/>
BY: William Kleitz - Chapter 5<br/>
_Detailed coverage of comparator design principles and their integration in digital processing systems._
